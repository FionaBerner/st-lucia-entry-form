# 🏝️ Caribbean Island Hopping From St. Lucia

St. Lucia is a great base for exploring nearby Caribbean islands. Many travelers combine St. Lucia with one or two additional destinations, depending on time, budget, and flight availability.

## Islands Commonly Visited From St. Lucia
These islands are the most popular for multi‑island trips:

- **Martinique** — A short ferry ride away; French Caribbean culture, beaches, shopping.
- **Dominica** — Known for waterfalls, hot springs, and nature; accessible by ferry or short flight.
- **Barbados** — Direct flights; great beaches, nightlife, and cultural sites.
- **St. Vincent & the Grenadines** — Ideal for sailing, island hopping, and quiet beaches.
- **Antigua** — Quick flights; famous for 365 beaches and sailing events.
- **Grenada** — Known for spices, waterfalls, and diving.

## Travel Options Between Islands
- **Regional flights** via LIAT, Caribbean Airlines, interCaribbean Airways.
- **Ferries** between St. Lucia, Martinique, and Dominica.
- **Private charters** for sailing or small‑plane transfers.
- **Catamaran cruises** for multi‑day sailing itineraries.

## Budget and Time Considerations
- **Short trips (1–3 days):** Martinique or Dominica via ferry; Barbados via short flight.
- **Medium trips (4–6 days):** St. Vincent & the Grenadines or Antigua.
- **Longer trips (1 week+):** Grenada or multi‑island sailing routes.

Typical costs:
- **Ferry:** Budget‑friendly.
- **Regional flights:** Moderate to high depending on season.
- **Private charters:** Premium option.

## Important Reminder
Foreign visitors must complete the [**St. Lucia Entry Form**](https://stluciaentryform.com/) before traveling to the island. This online form is required for smooth, visa‑free entry into St. Lucia.
